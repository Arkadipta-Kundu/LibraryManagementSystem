Frequently Asked Questions

How do I borrow a book?

Simply log in to your account, search for the book you wish to borrow, and click the "Borrow" button.

What happens if I return a book late?

Our system automatically applies a late fee based on your library's policies. You can view penalties in your account.

Can I renew my borrowed books?

Yes, you can renew your borrowed books as long as they are not reserved by another user.

How do I create an account?

You can create an account by clicking the "Sign Up" button on our homepage and filling out the registration form.
